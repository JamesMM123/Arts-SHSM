program requirements

in the arts and culture shsm if you wish to pass you need 4 arts and and culture major credits 1 english credit in grade 11 or 12 1 business study or world studies credit 2 co op credits (completed either by half day co op or full day co op)

addional requirements

in the arts and culture shsm the other aditional requirement is: compulsory certifications: standard first aid,whmis,cpr level c
