program requirements

in the arts and culture shsm if you wish to pass you need the following:

4 arts and and culture major credits

1 english credit in grade 11 or 12

1 business study or world studies credit

2 co op credits (completed either by half day co op or full day co op)

addional requirements

in the arts and culture shsm the other aditional requirement is:

for compulsory certifications: standard first aid,whmis,cpr level c
